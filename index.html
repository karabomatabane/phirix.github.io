About Me
Intro
Hello, I'm George, a UX/UI designer with a strong focus on crafting exceptional user experiences. I am passionate about designing apps and websites that not only meet but exceed user expectations. My design philosophy is centered around incorporating user insights and UX best practices to create visually stunning user interfaces. As a UX professional, I am committed to delivering products that not only look great but also function seamlessly, making the user journey as intuitive and delightful as possible.
Experience
2 years experience
I have two years and counting experience in the UX design field. I have worked on a number of unique, amazing and challenging projects. Projects ranging from helpful student apps, E- commerce platforms that cater to the SMEs to unique ideas from starts-ups.
